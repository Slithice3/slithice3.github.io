Who am I?

This could either go very philosophical, or it can be really simple...Let's go with the simple way :3

 

When I was a kid, I've always been fascinated by the idea of internet...It was one of those things that really intrigues me. I was born in China, in a small village. Computer to me when I was young, was something that really no one can afford. Back in my day in my land, we still need to dial to get internet connections. I was lucky that one of my cousin had a computer, therefore I could experience internet way before any of my friends did.

 

Hey, did I mention I'm Ming? I'm a computer science major who likes cooking and my goal in undergraduate is to become a Vet. And no, becoming a vet has nothing to do with neither cooking nor computer science.

 

Snow makes everything better.

UB, hmm. Many people came to UB for different reasons. For me, I simply like the snow here. I was born in the southern part of China, hence I never get to see many blizzards, or any snowflakes at all. Later I moved to New York... Beautiful city, yet I feel like it's missing something. Later I found out what is really missing: the isolation and freedom. Here at UB, I get to sit outside and simply look at the lake, at the sky without any distractions, isn't that amazing.
